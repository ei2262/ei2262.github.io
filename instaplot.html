Instacart Dashboard

We are going to look at Instacart data, which is an online grocery service that allows you to shop online from local stores.

Graph of Popular Chain Restaurants in NYC

data(rest_inspec)

boro_rest = rest_inspec %>% 
 select(boro, violation_description, action, score, dba, cuisine_description, inspection_type, critical_flag) %>% 
    filter(
    dba %in% c("DUNKIN' DONUTS", "STARBUCKS", "BURGER KING", "SWEETGREEN", "SUBWAY", "MCDONALD'S", "PANERA BREAD", "PARIS BAGUETTE", "PRET A MANGER", "FRESH & CO")
  ) %>% 
  drop_na(score)

boro_rest %>%
  count(dba) %>% 
  mutate(dba = fct_reorder(dba, n)) %>% 
  plot_ly(x = ~dba, y = ~n, color = ~dba, type = "bar", colors = "viridis")
